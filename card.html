Panda

Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm – that’s about the size of a pencil! They are also born blind and only open their eyes six to eight weeks after birth.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Lenght: 1.2-1.5 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: large bamboo forests

Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears. In the wild, they are found in thick bamboo forests, high up in the mountains of central China. they eat a lot they eat 12 KG of bambo in a day
